05 — Operating Principles
§ 05 / 05

Five rules. Break them only if you can write a postmortem.

P / 01

Lab over agency

We are a research instrument, not a brand. Every artifact should look like it was printed on a serial printer that's seen things.

P / 02

One signal color

Phosphor amber leads. Everything else is substrate. If your design needs three accents to work, the design isn't working.

P / 03

Numerals are content

Run IDs, timestamps, version strings, percentages — surface them. The math is the mood.

P / 04

Show the seams

Scanlines, grain, monospace alignment, dotted rules. Polish without sterility. The hand of the operator should be visible.
